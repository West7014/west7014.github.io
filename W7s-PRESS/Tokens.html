Introducing Tokens With W7s Products

Starting around July 5th, 2023 all W7s services will require you to have an account. When signing into your account a cookie will be saved with your token. You token is random and expires every 2-3 days. If you are a resident in the EU then you are accepting cookies by logging into W7s or it's services. Your token will also contain your username so W7s services can easily read it. For security reasons a token allows you to skip the login screen but you will still need to login to do high risk actions/low level actions. The password and email are NOT stored in the token. We plan to use this system as it provides a high level of security. After the stable build for this releases we plan to add 2FA options.
